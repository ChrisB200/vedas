Book 1, Hymn 1

agním īḷe puróhitaṃ yájnasya devám r̥tvíjam |

I praise Agni, the chosen priest, God, minister of sacrifice, The hotar, lavishest of wealth.

Book 1, Hymn 2

váyuṃ váyav īḷe |

I praise Vayu, the lord of winds.

Book 1, Hymn 3

áśvinā devarājaṃ námasyaṃ kṣetrāya bíbhr̥táṃ purá |

I praise the Ashvins, royal gods worthy of honor, bearing the chariot.
